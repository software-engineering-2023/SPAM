Credit Card Management Details:

Card Transactions: allows customers to access and review the details of their credit card activities. This feature provides a summary of all the transactions made using the credit card within a specific period.

Card Points: The primary purpose of card points is to reward customers for their card usage and encourage them to continue using the card for future transactions. Customers have the opportunity to reedem those points to an amount of cash to use them in stores.

Card Bills: are statements issued by cardholders detailing the transactions, charges, and payments associated with their credit card account during a specific billing cycle. It's a denote used for a credit card to cover a portion of expenses or bills. it means that you decide to use a credit card to pay a portion of a bill or expense rather than paying the full amount in cash, a cheque, or another method. it's a credit card to cover the entire amount of a bill or expense. Instead of using cash, check, or other payment methods, you choose to make the full payment using your credit card.

Report Card Issue: You can either report for a theft , loss , or damage to recieve a new credit card. Theft of a credit card refers to the unauthorized act of taking someone else's credit card without their permission and using it for fraudulent purposes. Loss of a credit card refers to the situation where you are no longer in possession of your credit card, either due to misplacement or unintentional separation. When we refer to "damage" in the context of a credit card, it typically refers to physical harm or impairment that affects the functionality or usability of the card.
